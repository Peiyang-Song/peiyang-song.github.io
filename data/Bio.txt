Peiyang Song is an undergraduate student studying Computer Science at California Institute of Technology (Caltech), advised by Prof. Steven Low, with a minor in Robotics advised by Prof. Günter Niemeyer. He is currently a researcher in the Stanford AI Lab (SAIL), advised by Prof. Noah Goodman and Gabriel Poesia. His research interest is mainly in machine reasoning, especially neuro-symbolic AI for mathematics and code generation. In the past, he also worked on energy-efficient machine learning systems and machine translation.
