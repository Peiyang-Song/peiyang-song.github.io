Peiyang Song is an undergraduate student studying Computer Science at California Institute of Technology (Caltech), advised by Prof. Steven Low, with a minor in Robotics advised by Prof. Günter Niemeyer. He is currently a researcher in the Stanford AI Lab (SAIL), advised by Prof. Noah Goodman and Gabriel Poesia. His current research interest is mainly in LLM reasoning, especially neuro-symbolic AI for formal math and verifiable code generation. In the past, he also published on neuro-symbolic methods for energy-efficient ML systems and neural machine translation.
